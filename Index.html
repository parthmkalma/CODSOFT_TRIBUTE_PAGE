Rohit Sharma

The Hitman of Indian Cricket

Biography

Rohit Gurunath Sharma (born 30 April 1987) is an Indian international cricketer and the current captain of the Indian national cricket team across all formats. A right-handed batsman, Sharma is widely regarded as one of the best batsmen of his generation and one of the greatest opening batsmen of all time. He is renowned for his timing, elegance, six-hitting abilities, and leadership skills.

Sharma holds several significant batting records, including:

  • Most sixes in international cricket
  • Most double centuries in ODI cricket (3)
  • Most centuries in Cricket World Cups (7)
  • Joint most hundreds in Twenty20 Internationals (6)
  • First player to score 6 T20I centuries

He plays for the Mumbai Indians in the IPL and represents Mumbai in domestic cricket. Under his captaincy, the Mumbai Indians won the IPL titles in 2013, 2015, 2017, 2019, and 2020, making him the most successful captain in IPL history, a record he shares with MS Dhoni.

Sharma was part of the Indian team that won the 2007 T20 World Cup and the 2013 ICC Champions Trophy, playing in the finals of both tournaments. He is one of four players to have participated in every edition of the ICC T20 World Cup, from the inaugural 2007 edition to the latest in 2022.

Rohit Sharma holds the world record for the highest individual score (264) in a One Day International (ODI) match and is the only player to have scored three double centuries in ODIs. He also holds the record for the most hundreds (five) in a single Cricket World Cup, earning him the ICC Men's ODI Cricketer of the Year award in 2019.

He has been honored with two national awards: the Arjuna Award in 2015 and the prestigious Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award in 2020 by the Government of India. Under his leadership, India won the 2018 and 2023 Asia Cups, both in the ODI format, and the 2018 Nidahas Trophy in T20I format.

Beyond cricket, Sharma is an active supporter of animal welfare campaigns. He is the official Rhino Ambassador for WWF-India and a member of People for the Ethical Treatment of Animals (PETA). He has worked with PETA to raise awareness about the plight of homeless cats and dogs in India.

Achievements

    First player to score five centuries in a single World Cup edition (2019)

    Rohit Sharma's exceptional performance in the 2019 ICC Cricket World Cup saw him become the first player to score five centuries in a single edition of the tournament. His runs were crucial for India's campaign in reaching the semi-finals.

    Most sixes in a Test series (13 sixes)

    During the 2019 Test series against South Africa, Rohit Sharma set a record for hitting the most sixes in a Test series. His aggressive batting style and ability to clear the boundary consistently made him a formidable batsman in the series.

    Fastest T20I century by an Indian (35 balls)

    Rohit Sharma's record-breaking century against Sri Lanka in 2017 is the fastest century by an Indian in T20 Internationals. His innings included an array of powerful shots and showcased his ability to dominate the opposition bowlers.

    Joint-most centuries in T20 Internationals (4 centuries)

    Rohit Sharma shares the record for the most centuries in T20 Internationals with Colin Munro. His ability to convert good starts into big scores has been instrumental in his success in the shortest format of the game.
